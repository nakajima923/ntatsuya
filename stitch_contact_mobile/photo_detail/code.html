Electric Midnight

Location

location_on

Shibuya, Tokyo, Japan

Equipment

photo_camera

Leica M11

lens

Summilux-M 35mm f/1.4 ASPH

Aperture f/2.8
Shutter 1/250s
ISO 400
Exposure -0.3ev

"The intersection is a pulse. Caught between the cyan of the billboards and the deep magenta of the rain-slicked pavement, the city feels like a living, breathing machine."

Captured during a late-night walk through Tokyo's busiest district. The goal was to isolate the movement of light rather than the people. The Leica M11’s sensor preserves every chromatic nuance of the neon signs without losing detail in the deepest shadows of the alleyways.
